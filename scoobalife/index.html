About Us

Our Diving base Calypso has more than 25 years of experience in SCUBA diving organisation. Our main goal is to provide diving courses for professional and for first-time divers supported by SSI franchise. Our diving courses consist of theory (we provide digital online literature) and diving lessons with equipment, instructor and transfer by boat to diving locations. Beside diving courses, we organize diving excursions and discovery diving (first-time diving). We offer over 20 different locations of shipwrecks, amphorae, reefs and underwater wells (Vrulja). Out of the season we provide wide range of inland / inshore diving operations: lifting operations, mooring construction, cutting, concreting and shipworks such as: hull inspection, corrosion inspection, underwater cleaning, prop polishing, pipeline construction and inspection. Right from the beginning, our company was engaged in cultural and natural heritage conservation and environmental preservation (see page Projects) what is becoming more and more our company policy.
